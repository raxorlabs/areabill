Delete AreaBill Account

Information about permanently deleting your AreaBill account and associated data.

Overview

AreaBill allows users to permanently delete their account directly from within the application.

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Once completed, access to account-related services will be removed.

How To Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the AreaBill app by following these steps:

  • Open AreaBill.
  • Go to Settings.
  • Open About.
  • Select Delete Account.
  • Review the information shown.
  • Press Delete Account.
  • Confirm the deletion request.

After confirmation, the account deletion process will begin automatically.

What Data Is Deleted

When an account is deleted, AreaBill may remove:

  • Google Sign-In account association.
  • Authentication information used by the app.
  • Stored login session information.
  • Cloud backup data associated with the account, where applicable.
  • Business profile information stored in cloud backup services.
  • Calculation records stored in cloud backup services.
  • Billing records stored in cloud backup services.

Local Device Data

Most calculations, bills, and customer records are stored locally on the user's device.

Deleting an account removes account access and authentication information. Users may additionally remove local application data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the application.

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent.

After an account has been deleted, it cannot be recovered and any deleted information may not be restorable.
