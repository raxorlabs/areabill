AreaBill Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

AreaBill respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how users can manage or delete their data.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address provided through Google Sign-In.
  • Calculation records, bills, customer information, and business profile information created by the user.
  • Subscription status for Premium features.

How Data Is Stored

For free users, calculations, bills, customer information, and settings are stored locally on the user's device.

For Premium users who choose to use Cloud Backup, selected data may be stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore under the user's account.

Cloud Backup

Cloud Backup is an optional Premium feature. Data is uploaded only when the user explicitly uses backup functionality.

Cloud Backup may include:

  • Calculations
  • Bills
  • Customer records
  • Business profile information

How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality.
  • Enable account sign-in.
  • Enable Premium cloud backup services.
  • Restore user data when requested by the user.
  • Provide customer support.

Data Sharing

AreaBill does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data may be processed through Google Firebase services solely for providing authentication and cloud backup features.

Account Deletion

Users may permanently delete their account directly inside the AreaBill application.

Navigation:

Settings → About → Delete Account

When an account is deleted:

  • The Firebase account is removed.
  • The user is signed out.
  • Local authentication data is removed.
  • Associated cloud backup data may also be removed where applicable.

Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to help protect user information and cloud-stored data.

Children's Privacy

AreaBill is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
