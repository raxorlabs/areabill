AreaBill Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

AreaBill respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use AreaBill, how that information is used, where it is stored, and the choices available to you regarding your data.

By using AreaBill, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

AreaBill may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address provided through Google Sign-In.
  • Calculation records created by the user.
  • Billing records generated by the user.
  • Customer information entered by the user.
  • Business profile information entered by the user.
  • Premium subscription status.

Google Sign-In

AreaBill allows users to sign in using their Google account.

When signing in, Google may provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if available)

This information is used only for authentication, account management, and Premium feature access.

Local Device Storage

Most AreaBill data is stored locally on the user's device.

This may include:

  • Calculations
  • Bills
  • Customer records
  • Business profile information
  • Application settings

Local data remains on the device unless the user deletes it, uninstalls the application, or chooses to use cloud backup features.

Premium Cloud Backup

AreaBill offers optional cloud backup functionality for Premium users.

Cloud backup is only performed when the user explicitly chooses to use backup functionality.

Cloud backup may include:

  • Calculations
  • Bills
  • Customer records
  • Business profile information

Cloud backup data is associated with the authenticated user account and stored using Google Firebase services.

How We Use Information

  • Provide application functionality.
  • Save and restore user data.
  • Enable cloud backup services.
  • Manage Premium subscriptions.
  • Authenticate users.
  • Improve reliability and user experience.
  • Provide customer support when necessary.

Third-Party Services

AreaBill may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Google Play Billing

These services operate under their own privacy policies and may process information necessary to provide their services.

Data Sharing

AreaBill does not sell, rent, or trade user information.

Information may be processed by trusted service providers such as Google Firebase solely for authentication, cloud backup, and subscription functionality.

No personal information is shared with advertisers.

Data Retention

User data is retained until:

  • The user deletes the data.
  • The user deletes their account.
  • The user uninstalls the application.
  • The user removes cloud backups where applicable.

Cloud-stored information remains associated with the user's account until deleted through available account management processes.

Account Deletion

Users can permanently delete their account directly within the AreaBill application.

Navigation:

Settings → About → Delete Account

When an account is deleted:

  • The user account is removed.
  • The user is signed out.
  • Authentication credentials are removed.
  • Local authentication data is cleared.
  • Associated cloud backup data may also be removed where applicable.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Security

AreaBill uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect user information and cloud-stored data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

However, no electronic storage or internet transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

AreaBill is not intended for children under the age of 13.

The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the application, legal requirements, or business practices.

Updated versions will be posted on this page together with the revised update date.
